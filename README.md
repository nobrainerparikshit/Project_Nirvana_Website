# Project Nirvana

> Transforming curiosity into innovation through research-driven STEM education.

Project Nirvana is a non-profit STEM education initiative dedicated to empowering students in Grades 6–10 with the knowledge, mentorship, and opportunities needed to become the next generation of innovators. Instead of focusing solely on teaching science, Nirvana builds structured pathways that enable students to explore, experiment, prototype, conduct research, and compete on national and international stages.

Our mission is simple:

> Innovation is far more widely distributed than opportunity.

---

## The Problem

India has one of the world's largest education systems, yet many students lack access to practical STEM education, mentors, research exposure, and innovation ecosystems. While initiatives such as Atal Tinkering Labs have expanded infrastructure, many remain underutilized due to the absence of structured mentorship and project-based learning.

Project Nirvana bridges this gap by creating an ecosystem where students learn through building, questioning, researching, and solving real-world problems.

---

# Our Vision

To ensure that every student, regardless of geography or socioeconomic background, has access to world-class STEM education, research opportunities, mentorship, and innovation pathways.

We believe talent is universal.

Opportunity is not.
